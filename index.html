About Mtokijani Foundation

The Mtokijani Foundation is an environmental NGO based in Tanzania and originating from Arusha. It focuses on reforestation, river cleanups, and sustainable water management. The Foundation organizes tree planting campaigns, builds and manages fish ponds for natural filtration, and restores degraded catchments to preserve ecosystems.

Our Activities

Tree Planting
Community and school planting to restore green cover.

River Cleanups
Mobilizing volunteers to remove pollution and protect waterways.

Waste Management
Recycling and cleanup campaigns to reduce pollution.

Beekeeping
Supporting sustainable apiculture and biodiversity.

Aquaponics
Combining fish farming and vegetable cultivation for efficiency.

Sustainable Agriculture
Training in organic and regenerative farming.

Restoration & Revegetation
Rehabilitating degraded lands with native species.

EcoGreen Projects
Promoting renewable energy and eco-friendly tech.

Mission

To promote environmental conservation, climate action, tree planting, waste management, and sustainable community livelihoods through education, advocacy, and innovation.

Vision

To create a sustainable and environmentally conscious world where every community values, restores, and protects natural ecosystems for present and future generations.
