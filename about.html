About Mtokijani Foundation

The Mtokijani Foundation is an environmental NGO based in Tanzania originating from Arusha. It focuses on reforestation, river cleanups, and sustainable water management. The Foundation works with communities to plant trees, clean rivers, and build fish ponds for natural filtration.

Mtokijani Worldwide

Mtokijani Worldwide is a green global movement expanding the foundation's work internationally. We mobilize communities, share knowledge, and implement sustainable projects worldwide.
